What is IRC?

IRC expands to "Internet Relay Chat" which represents a protocol that faciliates communication between users real time. Its an open and decenteralised system that is still used by many people for communication within the open source community each other. There are many irc servers you can connect to and each server has many channels prefixed by a "#" that you can join. Channels are created based on subjects of interest. There are a few things to know before exploring irc.

  • Channels prefixed by a "#" denote official channels and ones with "##" are unoffical
  • You can join any public channel but a few of them require invites or keys to enter
  • If you have sent a query to a channel patiently wait for the response
  • Its best to not directly pm anyone unless they explicitly ask you to
  • You can search for channels in Libera using the alis bot. Do "/query alis"
